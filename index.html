Textbook Market

Problem

  • Physical textbooks are very expensive (often >$100 per book).
  • After the course is finished, the textbook doesn't get used anymore and takes up space on the bookshelf.

Solution

Create an application that allows BMCC students to buy & sell used textbooks

  • Users can post books for sale.
  • Users can view the books that other users have put up for sale, including details like the title, author, edition, and course.
  • If users don't see the book they want, they can post a request for that book.
